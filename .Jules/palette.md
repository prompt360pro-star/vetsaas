## 2025-02-14 - Accessible Form Inputs and Descriptions\n**Learning:** Form components that lack a reliable `id` can fail to properly associate `<label>` elements and contextual descriptions (like hints or error messages) to their inputs. We learned that utilizing `useId()` offers a reliable fallback to connect labels via `htmlFor`, and error/hint texts via `aria-describedby`, ensuring screen reader users clearly understand form context and validation states without requiring manual IDs for every input.\n**Action:** Apply the `useId()` fallback pattern and dynamic `aria-describedby` configurations to all custom input components (Input, Select, Textarea) moving forward to prioritize accessibility out-of-the-box.
